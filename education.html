Wine Education

Wine Education for the South Asian Palate

Wine education should not begin with memorizing European regions.

For South Asian consumers, wine education should begin with the food they already understand.

At Tikka Wines, our education programs will teach wine through familiar reference points:

  • Why sweetness helps with spice
  • Why heavy tannins can clash with chili
  • Why rosé is so useful with mixed Indian meals
  • Why aromatic whites work with masala
  • Why dessert wines pair beautifully with mithai
  • How to serve wine at Indian dinner parties
  • How to pair wine with vegetarian food
  • How to select wines for weddings and celebrations

Education Programs

1. Wine 101 for Indian Food Lovers

A beginner-friendly introduction to wine using South Asian dishes as the teaching framework.

2. Spice & Wine Workshops

A guided tasting showing how chili, cumin, cardamom, clove, turmeric, tamarind, yogurt, and ghee interact with wine.

3. Restaurant Pairing Programs

Designed for Indian restaurants, caterers, chefs, and hospitality teams.

4. Home Entertaining Guides

Simple wine menus for:

  • Diwali dinners
  • Wedding events
  • Family parties
  • Weekend dinners
  • Tasting nights

5. Wine Tours

Curated, expert-led vineyard tours and wine experiences in relevant AVA regions, nationally and abroad.
