The Tikka Wines Portfolio

Our launch portfolio is built around the real structure of the South Asian table.

South Asian meals rarely consist of one dish. A single table may include fried snacks, chutneys, curries, rice, grilled meats, lentils, breads, pickles, sweets, and yogurt. That requires a portfolio—not a single “perfect” wine.

Our five launch wines are designed to cover the full spectrum:

Silver Trunk White

Gewürztraminer-based white

Aromatic spice, rich curries, tandoori flavors
