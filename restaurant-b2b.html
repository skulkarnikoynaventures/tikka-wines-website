For Restaurants, Caterers & Retailers

Tikka Wines helps South Asian restaurants and retailers solve a practical problem:

What wine should we recommend with Indian food?

Most menus either avoid wine pairings or rely on generic bottles. Tikka Wines gives restaurants and retailers a dedicated portfolio with clear pairing logic.
