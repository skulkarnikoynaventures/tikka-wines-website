About Us

A New Wine Category for the South Asian Table

Tikka Wines is a wine brand owned by Savora Beverages LLC, created to serve a clear and underserved need: wines thoughtfully crafted and curated for the South Asian palate.

We are not trying to force South Asian food into traditional wine-pairing rules.

We are building a new framework.

South Asian cuisine deserves wines that understand its complexity—wines that can move from spice to richness, from street food to celebration, from casual dinner to wedding table.

Our Mission

To make wine more enjoyable, more intuitive, and more culturally relevant for South Asian consumers and food lovers.

Our Vision

To become the leading wine brand for the global South Asian community.

A brand known for:

  • Thoughtful pairing
  • Approachable education
  • Global sourcing
  • Cultural authenticity
  • Celebration
  • Taste

Beyond the Bottle

Tikka Wines is not only a wine label.

It is a wine experience platform.

We intend to build the brand through:

  • Wine tasting programs
  • South Asian food pairing events
  • Wine education
  • Restaurant partnerships
  • Wine tours in Europe and other wine regions
  • Expert-led experiences

This mirrors the business plan’s goal of offering complementary activities such as wine tours, tastings, education, and expert-led programs.
